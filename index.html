Design & Engineering for Making (DE4M) Lab

My research in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) focuses on integrating tangible user interfaces, wearable technology, and artificial intelligence into everyday human experiences. I develop interactive systems that bridge physical and digital interactions.

I specialize in prototyping hardware-software systems, sensor integration, and AI applications for interactive experiences. My methodology combines qualitative approaches (interviews, observational studies) with quantitative techniques (sensor data analysis, surveys, experimental evaluation) through lab and field studies.

I am advised by Prof. Liang He at the DE4M Lab, UTD.
