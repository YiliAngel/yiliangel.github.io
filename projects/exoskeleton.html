Ongoing
Duration
Oct 2025 – Present
Lab
DE4M Lab, UTD

Overview

This ongoing project explores a brain-computer interface (BCI) pipeline that connects neural intention decoding with a wearable hand exoskeleton, aimed at helping Spinal Cord Injury (SCI) patients relearn functional hand skills for Activities of Daily Living. A workshop paper on this work has been accepted at CHI '26.

More details will be available after publication.
