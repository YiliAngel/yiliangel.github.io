Sustainable House: A Combination of TUI and GUI for Renewable Energy Education

Type
Hardware Project
Duration
3 weeks
Role
Designer & Developer
Tech
Processing, Arduino, Figma

Overview

A zero-energy house model demonstrating renewable energy integration through a combined GUI and physical model. Users interact digitally to select energy systems (solar, wind, geothermal) and see corresponding physical responses in a miniature hardware model.

Physical Components

  • Wind turbine with LEDs and motor, simulating electricity generation
  • Solar panels tracking light via dual ambient light sensors and servo motor
  • Heat pump visualized with neon light tubes

Design Question

"How might we design an interactive and tangible learning experience to help students understand and appreciate the role of renewable energy in creating a sustainable future?"
