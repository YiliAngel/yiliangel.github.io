Human-AI Co-Writing in Knowledge-Intensive Tasks: Behaviors, Challenges, and Collaboration Patterns

Status
In Submission
Duration
Jan – Aug 2025
Role
Researcher
Methods
Within-subject Study, Qualitative Analysis

Overview

This study investigates how people collaborate with large language models (LLMs) during knowledge-intensive writing tasks. Through a within-subject user study with 23 participants, we identify distinct collaboration behaviors and synthesize patterns that reflect how personal intentions shape the use of AI in knowledge work.

Research Contributions

  • Coordinated and conducted a within-subject user study with 23 participants to evaluate LLM-human collaboration patterns
  • Analyzed extensive qualitative data from writing activity logs and interviews to identify distinct collaboration behaviors
  • Synthesized collaboration patterns that reflect how personal intentions shape the use of AI in knowledge work
